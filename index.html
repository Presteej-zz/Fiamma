CHINA

The name alone makes you want to get ready to pack. It's hard to know what a place like this is like without actually traveling there. If you are looking to au pair in a destination that is exotic, packed full of new sights and experiences, stimulating, and overall different, China is the right place.

“He who returns from a journey is not the same as he who left.” — Chinese proverb

Explore the history of a country that continues to withstand the test of time.

IMMERSE YOURSELF IN THE EXPERIENCE OF A LIFETIME

One of the Four Ancient Civlilations, China boasts one of the richest breadths of culture with a history spanning over 5,000 years. From its exquisite delicacies to its unparalled natural beauty and warm, hospitable people, China will leave you with a repeatedly satisfied stomach, a fulfulled sense of wanderlust, and unforgettable memories to go along with it all.

Still not sure? Click here to read more about why you should consider being an au pair in China.

LIVE AN AUTHENTIC CHINESE LIFE

Live, eat, sleep, and breathe Chinese culture once you're paired with one of our welcoming and compassionate families. Each potential family you encounter will have been carefully vetted through us and screened according to your preferences to ensure that you are provided with a wholesome environment and open cultural experience, not an employer/employee relationship. With our families, you are family.

Check out what some of our past and current au pairs have to say about their experiences in China.

LEARN THE LANGUAGE SPOKEN BY THE MOST PEOPLE IN THE WORLD

Add to your arsenal a language that not only is one of the six official languages of the United Nations, but a language spoken by 16% of the world's total population, as you use it to communicate and navigate your new temporary home. Whether it's to boost your career prospects, tackle a new challenge in the form of mastering Chinese characters, forge new relationships, or even just ask for directions to the Great Wall or the Shenzhen/Hong Kong borders, your horizons will be broadened with each and every word you learn along the way.

Learn more about how being an au pair with Fiamma can help you with your Chinese language goals.

MAKE LIFELONG FRIENDS AND FAMILY

You're not just coming and then leaving- you're forging lifelong connections. You're becoming someone's big brother or sister, friend, teacher, and son or daughter to parents who will love you for the rest of your life. The Fiamma au pair experience doesn't end simply because you're no longer in China- you will always have a family there.
